Hello! Before you enter my den, there's a few things you should know.

This site was designed for computers, and will never support mobile. It was developed on Firefox, with some additional testing in Opera. I use a 1440p screen, but I made sure it looks nice in 1080p as well.

This site has a stormy weather background, with rain and lightning effects. Rain and lightning can be toggled using the Settings panel on the right side of the home page.
